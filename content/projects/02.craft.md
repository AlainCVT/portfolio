**Craft** was one of the _dApps_ developed at **Staky**.
The project was built on the Korean **ICON** blockchain.
It had about _10,000 weekly users_ and _$2M in volume_.

**Yoon Suk-yeol**, former South Korean president, issued an _NFT_ on live <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x-EBYnqQ0_I&t=4320s" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="MBCNEWS">Korean national television</a> using Craft.
