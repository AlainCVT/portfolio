**Victor Mertz** is a former student at <a href="https://www.hetic.net/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" title="HÉTIC">HÉTIC</a>, and the founder of **Offit**, a B2B sports coaching service.
To support the growth of his business, he submitted his project to the school so that current students could design and develop a website as part of their coursework.

The project was assigned to me and some of my classmates, and we worked on it as a team.
We were responsible for defining all the _content_ and _features_ needed to build the website, and how Victor can manage the content on his own.
